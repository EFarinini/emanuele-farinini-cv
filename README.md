# Emanuele Farinini, PhD - Curriculum Vitae

Personal CV website of Dr. Emanuele Farinini, chemometrician and consultant specializing in experimental design, multivariate analysis, image analysis, and software development (PyCAT).

## Live site

[efarinini.github.io](https://efarinini.github.io/emanuele-farinini-cv/)

## About

Dr. Emanuele Farinini is a chemometric consultant based in Genoa, Italy, working in collaboration with Prof. Riccardo Leardi. His expertise covers experimental design (DoE), multivariate analysis, process monitoring and optimization, multivariate image analysis, and software development. He is the lead developer of PyCAT (Python Chemometric Agile Tool) and co-developer of CAT. Since 2026, he serves as a representative of the Young Chemometricians Group within the Gruppo Tematico di Chemiometria, Societa Chimica Italiana (SCI).
